ICE SKATING

Ice Skating works nearly every muscle group in the body, and gliding requires synchronized movement of the legs, which is important for joint flexibility, It also builds up the leg and abdominal muscles. Like any workout, skating is great for cardiovascular health, it gets blood pumping and the heart rate up.
